CompTIA A+

CompTIA A+ is the industry’s leading entry‑level credential for IT support professionals. It validates your ability to troubleshoot, configure and maintain hardware, software and operating systems, and lays the groundwork for a successful IT career.

Our training explores PC assembly, mobile devices, virtualization, cloud computing and basic networking. You’ll develop the hands‑on skills needed to diagnose problems, implement security best practices and support users across platforms.

After completing the course you’ll be prepared to take the CompTIA A+ Core 1 (220‑1101) and Core 2 (220‑1102) exams, opening pathways into help desk, technical support and IT operations roles.
