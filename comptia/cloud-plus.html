CompTIA Cloud+

CompTIA Cloud+ shows you have the technical skills to design, deploy and secure cloud environments that support business operations. It is the only cloud certification that covers multi‑cloud and hybrid solutions across vendors.

In this course you’ll learn how to analyze system requirements, implement workload migrations, manage automation and orchestration, and troubleshoot cloud architectures. You’ll also develop expertise in high availability, disaster recovery and performance tuning.

With our training you’ll be prepared to pass the Cloud+ (CV0‑003) exam and pursue roles such as cloud systems administrator, cloud engineer or DevOps specialist.
