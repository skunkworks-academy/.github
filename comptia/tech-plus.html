CompTIA Tech+

CompTIA Tech+ is your launchpad into the world of technology. It covers foundational concepts in computing, networking, software development, data management and cybersecurity so you can discover where your passion lies.

Through interactive labs and real‑world examples you’ll gain a broad understanding of how modern technologies work together. Whether you’re considering a tech career or looking to build a strong baseline for further certifications, Tech+ provides the context you need.
