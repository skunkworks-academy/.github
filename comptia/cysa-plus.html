CompTIA CySA+

CompTIA Cybersecurity Analyst (CySA+) proves that you can proactively defend and continuously improve the security posture of an organization. It focuses on threat detection and response rather than simple compliance, helping you develop real‑world security analyst skills.

In this course you’ll master security analytics, apply behavioral analytics to detect threats, analyze and interpret data, and configure and use threat detection tools. You’ll also learn to perform vulnerability management and assist in incident response and compliance monitoring.

After completing our training you’ll be prepared for the CySA+ (CS0‑003) exam and ready for roles such as SOC analyst, security engineer or compliance analyst.
