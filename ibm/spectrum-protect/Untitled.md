## Introduction

Welcome to the official preparation guide for the IBM Spectrum Protect V8.1.12 Implementation exam (C1000-137). This document is designed to provide candidates with the necessary knowledge and skills to successfully fulfill their role in IBM Spectrum Protect environments. This guide will cover key exam topics, recommended skills, requirements, and exam objectives.

### Recommended Skills

Before attempting the IBM Spectrum Protect V8.1.12 Implementation exam, candidates should have the following skills and knowledge:

- Backup methodologies
- Storage device technology
- Operating systems (Windows, Linux, UNIX)
- Virtualization
- Networking
- Disaster recovery
- Encryption techniques
- IBM Spectrum Protect maintenance and scripting
- Applications/databases backup
- Bare machine recovery
- Snapshot technologies
- IBM DB2 for Spectrum Protect
- Cloud capabilities
- IBM Spectrum Protect Plus

### Exam Details

- **Number of questions:** 61
- **Number to pass:** 39
- **Time allowed:** 90 minutes
- **Status:** Live
